Esto instalará el Homebrew Channel en la RedNAND, tras lo cual no requerirás del navegador de internet para iniciar el Homebrew Launcher.
{: .notice--info}

#### Instrucciones

1. Inicia el Homebrew Launcher en la RedNAND usando `http://loadiine.ovh`
2. Inicia WUP Installer - Mod Y
3. Press (A) to install the Homebrew Channel
4. Press (Home) to exit the installer once it has completed installing

___

You can now launch the Homebrew Launcher on RedNAND just by launching the Homebrew Channel.
{: .notice}
